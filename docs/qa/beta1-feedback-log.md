## Scope Guard

Mark the following as `out-of-scope-v1.1` unless the PRD is deliberately
changed:

- accounts or Google login;
- permanent usernames;
- match history;
- leaderboard;
- replay viewer;
- English UI, `/en` routes, or a language toggle;
- chat or spectating;
- tournaments;
- ads, sponsors, payments, affiliate links, or monetization placeholders.
